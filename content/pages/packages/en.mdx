## Package options

### Launch Site

For new businesses that need a clear online presence fast.

- One main message.
- Bilingual navigation.
- Contact CTA and basic analytics.

### Conversion Site

For brands that want SEO, content, and a stronger lead pipeline.

- Multi-page structure.
- Blog-ready foundation.
- Better event tracking.

### Web + Mobile Platform

For teams that need something beyond a brochure site.

- Custom product logic.
- Mobile-ready flows.
- Room to grow over time.

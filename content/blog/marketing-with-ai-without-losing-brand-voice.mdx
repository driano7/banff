# Marketing with AI without losing your brand voice

AI should make your marketing faster, not generic. The brand still needs a point of view.

## Define the rules first

Before using AI, document the basics:

- Tone of voice.
- Words to use often.
- Words to avoid.
- The kind of proof your brand can show.

## Use AI for the first draft

AI is excellent for structure, idea generation, and variation. It is not the final editor.

## Add human review

The final pass should check:

1. Accuracy.
2. Specificity.
3. Brand consistency.
4. Local relevance.

> The strongest workflow is AI plus judgment, not AI instead of judgment.

## Final takeaway

Use AI to remove friction from the process, then use people to keep the message sharp, believable, and on brand.
